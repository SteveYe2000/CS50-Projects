About Us

Ansel Adams

We are a team of professional photographers with decades of industry experiences based in New York. Established in 1998. Our company is established in honor of the world's renowed photographer Ansel Adams who is our former CEO of the company. He is now retired and left the company for new up coming generations. To carry out his famous legacy, we mainly focus on the needs of our customers and deliver the highest quality services. Services include weddings, graduations, sports, press and other special osscasions. We also offer different types of photography services tailored to your special requirements.
